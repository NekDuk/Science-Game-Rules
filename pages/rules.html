How to play the game

  • 1. Each player will roll a dice to determine the order of the player. The player with the highest number on the die goes first and so on.
  • 2. On a players turn, they will roll the die to determine how many times they can move, after rolling, they will spin a wheel to determine the direction they will move in.
  • 3. After rolling and spinning, players will the move in the direction they spun. Each square will have a special effect. check special mechanics.
  • 4. After a player is done doing what the square does, the next player will move.
  • 5. This will repeat until the game ends.
