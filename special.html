Special Mechanics

Squares

There are 4 types of squares. Landing on a square will trigger its effect.

Blank Square

Indicated by a blank or empty white/yellow square. Landing on this square does nothing.

Q & A Square

Indicated by a square with a blue question mark. Players who landed on this square will be given a question to answer.

There are 3 levels of questions indicated by its color:

  • Red - Difficult Question - 6 Electrons
  • Yellow - Intermediate Question - 4 Electrons
  • Green - Easy Question - 2 Electrons

If a player answers a question correctly, the will gain the amount of electrons indicated.

If a player answers incorrectly, they will return back to the square they came from.
